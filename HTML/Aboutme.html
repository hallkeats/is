About Me

My Story

Family

I grew up in Aurora, CO in a family of seven. I have three older sisters and a younger brother. My siblings and I were always close growing up, and now that we are all adults, we are still close. They all live in Utah, so we get together often. My parents always encouraged me to work hard. I am grateful for the way they pushed me to achieve greatness in every activity I pursued. I am grateful for them.






Fun

Sports have always been a part of my life. I grew up playing football, basketball, and lacrosse competitively. During my junior year of high school I fell in love with lifting weights. Now, I play sports, but not as often, and I go to the gym every day. Recently, I started bowling with my friends. I have gotten pretty good at it too. My high score is 207. I love to spend time wiht my friends doing any kind of activty, as long as it is not watching a movie. I love living in Provo because there are so many people and so many things to do.






Faith

Religion has shaped my life. I grew in a Latter-day Saint home. My parents taught me to pray and read scriptures every night. That habit has stuck with me and helped me to develop a relationship with my Heavenly Father. I know Jesus Christ is my Savior. Through him we are saved.
